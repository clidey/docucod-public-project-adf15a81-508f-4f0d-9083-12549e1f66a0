# Installing uBO Scope

This guide walks you through installing uBO Scope, a browser extension that reveals all remote server connections your browser attempts or completes. You'll find direct links to the official extension stores for supported browsers and tips to ensure a smooth installation.

---

## Supported Browsers

uBO Scope is available for the following browsers:

- **Chromium-based browsers (e.g., Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge)**
- **Mozilla Firefox**
- **Safari**

Before proceeding, confirm your browser version meets the minimum compatibility requirements:

- **Chromium**: Version 122 or later
- **Firefox**: Version 128 or later
- **Safari**: Version 18.5 or later

See the [Prerequisites & System Requirements](./prerequisites-system-requirements) page for more details.

---

## Step-by-Step Installation Instructions

### 1. Installing on Chromium Browsers

1. Open your Chromium-based browser.
2. Navigate to the Chrome Web Store page for uBO Scope:

   [https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ubo-scope/bbdpgcaljkaaigfcomhidmneffjjjfgp](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ubo-scope/bbdpgcaljkaaigfcomhidmneffjjjfgp)

3. Click **Add to Chrome**.
4. Confirm the installation by clicking **Add Extension** in the pop-up prompt.
5. Upon successful installation, the uBO Scope icon will appear in your browser toolbar.

### 2. Installing on Mozilla Firefox

1. Launch Firefox.
2. Visit the Firefox Add-ons page for uBO Scope:

   [https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ubo-scope/](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ubo-scope/)

3. Click **Add to Firefox**.
4. Confirm permissions by clicking **Add** and **Okay, Got It** if prompted.
5. The extension’s icon will show up in the toolbar once installed.

### 3. Installing on Safari

1. Open Safari.
2. Go to the Mac App Store Safari Extensions page or use the direct link when available.
3. Search for "uBO Scope" and select the extension.
4. Click **Get** and then **Install**.
5. After installation, enable the extension via Safari > Preferences > Extensions.
6. The uBO Scope icon will then be accessible in your Safari toolbar.

> **Note:** Safari requires an explicit permissions enablement step after installation.

---

## What to Expect After Installation

- A toolbar icon labeled **uBO Scope** will appear.
- The icon’s badge will display the count of distinct third-party remote servers your browser connected to.
- Clicking the icon opens a popup panel with detailed, categorized connection information.

---

## Tips for First-Time Extension Users

- **Pin the Extension Icon:** On some browsers, newly installed extensions can be hidden from the toolbar. Pin uBO Scope for easy access.
- **Permissions Prompt:** You may be prompted to grant permissions such as access to storage and webRequest monitoring. These are necessary for uBO Scope to function correctly.
- **Browser Restart:** If the icon or popup does not appear immediately, restart your browser to ensure proper service worker initialization.

---

## Verifying Installation

1. Visit a variety of websites.
2. Click the uBO Scope icon.
3. Confirm that a list of domains is displayed, categorized as:
   - Not blocked (allowed) domains
   - Stealth-blocked domains
   - Blocked domains
4. Observe the badge count updating as new third-party connections occur.

If you see these signs, uBO Scope is installed and operational.

---

## Common Installation Issues

- **Extension Icon Missing:** Pin the extension using your browser’s extension menu.
- **Permissions Not Granted:** Without permissions (activeTab, storage, webRequest), the extension cannot report connections accurately.
- **Browser Version Outdated:** Update your browser to meet minimum version requirements.

For detailed troubleshooting, see [Troubleshooting Common Issues](./troubleshooting-common-issues).
