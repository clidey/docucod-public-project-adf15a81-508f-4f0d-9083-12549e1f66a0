# Installing the Extension

Welcome to the first step in your journey with **uBO Scope**. This guide provides clear, step-by-step instructions to install the extension across supported browsers: **Chrome**, **Firefox**, and **Safari**. By following these straightforward directions, you'll have uBO Scope enabled and ready to reveal your browser's remote server connections.

---

## Supported Browsers and Key Notes

- **Google Chrome** (version 122.0 and above)
- **Mozilla Firefox** (version 128.0 and above)
- **Apple Safari** (version 18.5 and above)

<u>Note:</u> Make sure your browser version meets or exceeds these minimums to ensure full compatibility and optimal operation of the extension.

---

## Installation Steps by Browser

### 1. Google Chrome

1. Open the **Chrome Web Store** by navigating to the [uBO Scope Chrome extension page](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ubo-scope/bbdpgcaljkaaigfcomhidmneffjjjfgp).
2. Click the **Add to Chrome** button.
3. In the confirmation prompt, click **Add extension**.
4. Once installed, the extension icon (uBO Scope logo) will appear next to the address bar.

### 2. Mozilla Firefox

1. Open the **Firefox Add-ons** site at [uBO Scope for Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ubo-scope/).
2. Click on **Add to Firefox**.
3. Confirm the installation by clicking **Add** in the pop-up.
4. The extension icon will then be visible near the toolbar.

### 3. Apple Safari

1. Open the **Safari Extensions** Gallery or visit the appropriate app store for Safari extensions on your Mac.
2. Search for **uBO Scope**.
3. Click **Install** or **Get** and follow the system prompts to add the extension.
4. Enable uBO Scope in Safari’s **Preferences > Extensions** section if it is not activated automatically.

---

## Verifying Installation

After installation on any browser, confirm the extension activation:

- Look for the **uBO Scope icon** in your browser toolbar (a stylized uBO logo).
- Click the icon to open the popup window. It should display a message like "NO DATA" initially (before browsing).
- When you visit websites, this popup will update to show connected domains.

<Tip>
If the icon does not appear, check your browser’s extension management page:

- **Chrome:** chrome://extensions/
- **Firefox:** about:addons
- **Safari:** Safari > Preferences > Extensions

Ensure uBO Scope is enabled.
</Tip>

---

## Using uBO Scope Right After Installation

1. Navigate to any website and observe the uBO Scope toolbar badge. It will display a number representing the count of distinct third-party domains your browser connected to.
2. Click the extension icon to open the detailed popup UI.
3. In the popup, you'll see three sections categorizing connections:
   - **Not Blocked:** Domains actively connected without any blocking
   - **Stealth-Blocked:** Domains where connections were silently redirected or interfered with
   - **Blocked:** Domains blocked due to errors or content blocking

This immediate feedback helps you audit your privacy exposure.

---

## Troubleshooting Installation Issues

- **Extension not appearing in toolbar:** Pin the extension icon manually via your browser's extension manager.
- **Browser too old / unsupported version:** Upgrade your browser to meet minimum version requirements.
- **Permission prompts declined:** Reinstall the extension and ensure to grant requested permissions (activeTab, storage, webRequest).

<Warning>
If you encounter repeated installation failures, ensure no conflicting extensions or policies are preventing installation.
</Warning>
