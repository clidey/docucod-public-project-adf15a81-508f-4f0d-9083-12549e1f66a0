# Installing uBO Scope

This guide provides you with clear, step-by-step instructions to install uBO Scope on your preferred browser—Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Whether you are new to browser extensions or seeking a streamlined installation experience, this page ensures a safe and successful setup.

---

## Supported Browsers

uBO Scope is compatible with the following browser environments:

- **Google Chrome** (version 122.0 or higher)
- **Mozilla Firefox** (version 128.0 or higher)
- **Safari** (version 18.5 or higher)

Ensure your browser is updated to meet or exceed these version requirements before installation.

---

## Prerequisites

Before installation, confirm the following:

- Your browser supports extensions and is updated to a supported version.
- You have access to the browser's extension/add-on store or can sideload extension packages.
- Your browser has permissions enabled to install extensions.

uBO Scope requires these permissions during installation to operate correctly:

- `webRequest` – To monitor network requests made by webpages.
- `activeTab` – To access information about the active tab.
- `storage` – To save session and configuration data.

These permissions are requested and granted through the browser during the installation process.

---

## Step-by-Step Installation

### 1. Installing on Google Chrome

1. Open the Chrome Web Store.

2. Visit the uBO Scope extension page:

   [Install uBO Scope for Chrome](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ubo-scope/bbdpgcaljkaaigfcomhidmneffjjjfgp)

3. Click the **Add to Chrome** button.

4. Review the requested permissions and click **Add extension** to confirm.

5. Upon successful installation, the uBO Scope icon will appear in your browser's toolbar.

6. Optional: Pin the extension for quick access by clicking the puzzle piece icon, then the pushpin next to uBO Scope.


### 2. Installing on Mozilla Firefox

1. Open the Firefox Add-ons site.

2. Visit the uBO Scope add-on page:

   [Install uBO Scope for Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ubo-scope/)

3. Click **Add to Firefox**.

4. Review the permissions and click **Add** to install.

5. The extension’s icon will appear near the address bar.

6. Pin the extension by opening the toolbar customization and dragging uBO Scope if desired.


### 3. Installing on Safari

1. Open the **Safari Extensions** page in the Mac App Store (Safari 18.5 or later required).

2. Search for "uBO Scope" or use the direct link (when available).

3. Click **Get** and then **Install** to add the extension to Safari.

4. Open Safari’s **Preferences** > **Extensions**, locate uBO Scope, and enable it.

5. The uBO Scope icon will be visible in Safari’s toolbar, allowing quick access.

---

## Verifying Installation

To verify that uBO Scope is installed and activated:

- Look for the uBO Scope icon in the browser toolbar.
- Click the icon to open the uBO Scope popup panel.
- You should see a message like "NO DATA" initially (until you visit a webpage).
- Visit a webpage, then open the panel again to observe real-time connection data.

If the icon or popup does not appear, review browser extension settings or restart the browser.

---

## Tips for a Smooth Installation

- **Ensure no conflicting extensions:** Some network-monitoring or content-blocking extensions may interfere with uBO Scope.
- **Avoid manual installs if unsure:** Prefer installing from official stores unless you are confident with developer mode.
- **Enable permissions promptly:** Allow all requested permissions to ensure full functionality.
