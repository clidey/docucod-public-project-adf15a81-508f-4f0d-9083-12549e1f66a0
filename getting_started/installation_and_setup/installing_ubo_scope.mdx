# Installing uBO Scope

Learn how to securely install uBO Scope on your preferred browser—Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. This guide walks you through finding the official installation source and completing the installation to ensure you gain immediate insight into third-party remote connections.

---

## 1. Choose Your Browser and Installation Source

uBO Scope is available for the following browsers:

- **Google Chrome** (Chromium-based browsers)
- **Mozilla Firefox**
- **Apple Safari**

Each browser’s installation link points to the official store or gallery, guaranteeing authenticity and security.

| Browser    | Official Installation Link                                    |
|------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| Chrome     | [Chrome Web Store · uBO Scope](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ubo-scope/bbdpgcaljkaaigfcomhidmneffjjjfgp) |
| Firefox    | [Firefox Add-ons · uBO Scope](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ubo-scope/)                |
| Safari     | Available on the Mac App Store (ensure Safari version ≥ 18.5) |


## 2. Installation Steps

Follow the specific instructions for your browser below.

### For Chrome (Chromium-based browsers):

1. Open the [Chrome Web Store page for uBO Scope](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ubo-scope/bbdpgcaljkaaigfcomhidmneffjjjfgp).
2. Click **Add to Chrome**.
3. When prompted, confirm by clicking **Add Extension**.
4. Wait for the extension to download and install.

### For Firefox:

1. Open the [Firefox Add-ons page for uBO Scope](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ubo-scope/).
2. Click **Add to Firefox**.
3. Confirm any permission dialogs by clicking **Add**.
4. Wait for the extension to install.

### For Safari:

1. Open the **Mac App Store** on your macOS device.
2. Search for **uBO Scope**.
3. Ensure your Safari version is at least 18.5 (check Safari > About Safari).
4. Click **Get** or **Install** to download and enable the extension.

> <Note>
> Safari requires a minimum version of 18.5 for uBO Scope; ensure your system is updated.
> </Note>

## 3. Verifying Installation Success

Once installed, confirm that uBO Scope is active and ready:

- **Toolbar Icon**: Look for the uBO Scope icon in your browser’s toolbar (usually near the URL bar).
- **Click the Icon**: Clicking it should open the uBO Scope popup panel which initially reads data for your current tab.
- **Badge Visibility**: The icon may display a badge showing the count of distinct third-party domains connected for the active tab (a lower count is desirable).


## 4. Tips for a Smooth Setup

- **Use Official Sources Only**: Installing extensions from the official stores ensures you receive secure, verified software updates.
- **Permissions**: uBO Scope needs permission to access `webRequest` data for URLs; approve these when prompted.
- **Browser Version**: For best results, use the latest stable version of your browser.
- **Restart Browser if Needed**: After installation, if the icon doesn't show, restart your browser to activate.

## 5. Troubleshooting Installation Issues

<AccordionGroup title="Common Installation Issues and Solutions">
<Accordion title="uBO Scope Icon Not Appearing in Toolbar">

1. Verify the extension is enabled in your browser’s extensions/add-ons page.
2. For Chrome and Firefox, check if the toolbar is customizable; you might need to pin the icon manually.
3. Restart your browser.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Installation Fails or Blocks Due to Permissions">

- Check your browser security settings or antivirus software to ensure it does not block extensions.
- Confirm you are installing from the official links to avoid tampering.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Safari Installation Unavailable">

- Confirm your macOS and Safari versions are up to date (Safari ≥ 18.5).
- Safari requires explicit enabling of extensions in Preferences > Extensions.

</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
