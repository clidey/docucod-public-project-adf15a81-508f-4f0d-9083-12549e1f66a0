# Installing uBO Scope on Your Browser

Welcome to the quick and effortless installation guide for uBO Scope. Whether you use Chromium, Firefox, or Safari, this page walks you through the exact steps needed to get uBO Scope running on your preferred browser. We’ll also connect you directly to the official extension stores to ensure you’re always installing the verified, latest version.

---

## 1. Supported Browsers and Versions

uBO Scope is designed to seamlessly integrate with the latest versions of the following browsers:

- **Chromium-based browsers** (Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, etc.) - Minimum Chromium version 122.0
- **Firefox** - Minimum Firefox version 128.0
- **Safari** - Minimum Safari version 18.5

Make sure your browser is up to date to guarantee optimal compatibility and security.

---

## 2. Installation Instructions

Follow the section that corresponds to your browser to install uBO Scope quickly.

### For Chromium and Chromium-Based Browsers

1. Open your Chromium-based browser.
2. Navigate to the [Chrome Web Store listing for uBO Scope](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ubo-scope/bbdpgcaljkaaigfcomhidmneffjjjfgp).
3. Click **Add to Chrome**.
4. Confirm the installation by clicking **Add extension** in the pop-up dialog.
5. After installation, you will see the uBO Scope icon appear in your toolbar next to the address bar.

### For Firefox

1. Open Firefox.
2. Visit the official [Firefox Add-ons page for uBO Scope](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/ubo-scope/).
3. Click **Add to Firefox**.
4. Confirm any prompts to install the extension.
5. The uBO Scope icon should now display in your toolbar.

### For Safari

1. Open Safari (ensure you have at least version 18.5).
2. Visit the Mac App Store and search for 'uBO Scope'.
3. Install the uBO Scope extension via the App Store.
4. Once installed, open Safari’s Preferences → Extensions.
5. Enable uBO Scope by checking its box.
6. The extension icon will now be accessible in your Safari toolbar.

---

## 3. Verifying Installation

Once installed, verify uBO Scope is active and ready to monitor connections:

- Look for the uBO Scope icon in your browser’s toolbar.
- Click the icon to open the extension popup.
- If the popup shows “NO DATA,” it means no network activity has been recorded yet. Navigate to a website to start seeing remote server connections.

<Tip>
If the icon does not appear automatically, check your browser’s extension management page and ensure uBO Scope is enabled.
</Tip>

---

## 4. What to Expect After Installation

- The toolbar icon badge will dynamically display the number of distinct third-party remote servers your browser connects to on the active tab.
- Clicking the icon will open a detailed popup showing domains with network requests classified as not blocked, stealth-blocked, or blocked.
- uBO Scope operates independently of other content blockers or DNS-based blocking, offering an accurate and real-time view of your browser’s network requests.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting Common Installation Issues

<AccordionGroup title="Troubleshooting Installation Problems">
<Accordion title="The extension icon is missing after install">
Verify that the extension is enabled in your browser’s settings. Sometimes the browser may hide new extensions behind a menu icon (… or puzzle icon). Pin the uBO Scope extension to your toolbar for easy access.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Installation fails or extension does not appear in the store">
Confirm your browser version meets the minimum requirements listed above. Older browsers may be blocked from installing this MV3-compliant extension.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Safari-specific Issues">
Make sure you have enabled the extension inside Safari Preferences → Extensions. Extensions installed from the App Store require explicit activation.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
